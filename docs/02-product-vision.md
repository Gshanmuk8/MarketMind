# 02 · Product Vision

## What MarketMind AI is

An **AI Strategic Intelligence Platform**. A founder enters their company website once; the platform understands the company, discovers its competitors, monitors the market continuously, and converts thousands of public signals into strategic conclusions: what changed, why it matters, whether to care, and what to do.

Reference shorthand: "Bloomberg Terminal + Perplexity + SimilarWeb + Crunchbase + AI Consultant" for startups. It is **not** a competitor tracker, not an analytics dashboard, not a feed.

## Who it serves

Founders, startup teams, and product leaders who currently burn hours across GitHub, Product Hunt, Reddit, LinkedIn, X, blogs, docs, pricing pages, release notes, AI announcements, news, and hiring pages — and still miss what matters.

## The bet

Founders don't need more information. They need an analyst who reads everything and explains it. MarketMind AI is that analyst, working 24/7, with perfect memory of the founder's own strategic decisions (see Decision Memory, doc 15).

## Differentiation

1. **Zero-effort setup** — one URL in, full competitive landscape out (~60 seconds).
2. **The Intelligence Layer** — no raw events; every signal carries "why it matters for MY company" and a recommended action.
3. **AI ecosystem tracking** — model releases, pricing, capabilities across OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Groq, Cerebras, OpenRouter, DeepSeek, Mistral, Meta, xAI, Perplexity — interpreted for the user's product.
4. **Trust tiers** — verified facts, AI inferences, and recommendations are never conflated.
5. **Decision platform, not monitoring platform** — intelligence flows into decisions (Decision Workspace) and decisions are remembered, revisited, and validated (Decision Memory).

## Long-term vision

The first thing a founder opens every morning — the way developers rely on GitHub Copilot. Not because it tells them everything, but because it tells them exactly what matters, remembers what they decided and why, and warns them when the market invalidates an old decision.

## Non-goals

- No scraping of private, gated, or unethically obtained data — public and legal sources only.
- No pretending to know private information (revenue, internal roadmaps) — inference is labeled inference.
- Light-only, editorial "Atelier" design language (owner decision, 2026-07; see doc 16). No generic dashboard aesthetics, no dark-SaaS clichés.
- No notification spam — the system is an assistant, not a newsletter.
